About Me

I am currently pursuing an M.S. in Electrical Engineering in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the South Dakota School of Mines & Technology (SDSMT). Previously, I completed an M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from SDSMT in May 2024. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology in 2013.

Following my undergraduate studies, I worked for eight years in various IT and telecommunications service providers, gaining hands-on experience in optical fiber networks, soft-switch systems, internet and telephone services, FTTX solutions, and cybersecurity.

During my graduate studies, I focused on machine learning, system identification of dynamical systems using machine learning techniquesthe, security of cyber-physical systems etc.
