MD KAUSAR HAMID MIJI

Md Kausar Hamid Miji is currently pursuing an M.S. in Electrical Engineering in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the South Dakota School of Mines & Technology (SDSM&T). He previously completed an M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from SDSM&T in May 2024. Mr. Kausar earned his bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology in 2013.

After completing his undergraduate studies, Mr. Kausar joined the IT and telecommunications industry, where he served in various roles for eight years. During this time, he gained extensive hands-on experience in optical fiber networks, soft-switch systems, internet and telephone services, FTTx solutions, and cybersecurity.

In his graduate studies, Mr. Kausar focused on machine learning, system identification of dynamical systems using data-driven techniques, and the security of cyber-physical systems.
